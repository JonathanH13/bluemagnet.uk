tACS Challenge Hardware

tACS Challenge is an international multi-centre initiative to test the real-time impact of Transcranial Alternating Current Stimulation on behaviours that result from phasic current modulation in the brain. Blue Magnet supplies purpose-built hardware for participating sites.
