About

Blue Magnet develops bespoke technology for medical research. We bridge the gap between complex experimental requirements and precision-engineered hardware — delivering solutions that are right first time.

Our clients include leading universities, NHS trusts, and international research institutes. We specialise in the demanding intersection of neuroimaging, electrophysiology, and neuromodulation research.
